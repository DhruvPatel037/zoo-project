Welcome to the Zoo Animals Page

Explore our animals, learn about their lives, and see their unique stories!

Bears

  • Ollie - A playful bear who loves fishing.

    • Ollie is known for his energetic dives into rivers to catch fish. He's a favorite among visitors for his curious and friendly demeanor.

  • Mona - A shy bear who enjoys quiet forests.

    • Mona spends most of her time in the woods, lounging under trees and nibbling on berries. She's calm and peaceful, preferring solitude.

Giraffes

  • Frankie - A friendly, loving long-neck baby.

    • Frankie is always curious about visitors, often bending down to greet them. He loves eating leaves and playing with his herd mates.

  • Coconut - A playful, energetic giraffe who loves people.

    • Coconut is a star at the zoo, entertaining everyone with her playful antics and love for snacking on treats from her keepers.

Lions

  • Mella - A lioness with a majestic presence.

    • Mella leads her pride with confidence and grace. She enjoys basking in the sun and roaring to assert her dominance.

  • Karl - A playful young lion who loves chasing his tail.

    • Karl is the most energetic of the pride. He's often seen pouncing on his toys and playfully wrestling with his siblings.

Monkeys

  • Cookie - A clever monkey with a talent for puzzles.

    • Cookie loves solving challenges set by her keepers. She's incredibly intelligent and loves climbing trees.

  • Earl - A mischievous monkey who loves stealing hats.

    • Earl is known for his antics, often sneaking up on visitors and playfully grabbing their hats. He keeps everyone entertained.

  • Banana Pudding - A monkey with a sweet tooth for bananas.

    • This lovable monkey earned his name for his obsession with bananas. He's always munching on one or hoarding them for later.

Alligators

  • Wren - A calm alligator who loves sunbathing.

    • Wren can be found lounging on the banks of the zoo's pond, enjoying the warmth of the sun and occasional swims.

  • Aspen - A curious alligator who loves exploring the water.

    • Aspen spends most of her time diving and searching for hidden treats placed by her keepers.

  • Mika - A speedy alligator known for her quick moves.

    • Mika is agile and fast, surprising everyone with her sudden bursts of energy when chasing her favorite toys.
